Create a VECTOR function that evaluates how well a location can support a functioning evil workforce including scientists, guards, technicians, and ninjas. Returns a probability distribution across 5 sustainability tiers.

Input Schema:
- location: A single location description (text, image, or video) representing one potential lair site

Evaluation Criteria (5 dimensions):

1. Living Quarters Potential:
- Capacity to house hundreds or thousands of personnel
- Quality of life sufficient to maintain morale
- Hierarchical accommodation: luxury for lieutenants, barracks for grunts
- Recreation facilities to prevent mutiny

2. Recruitment Pipeline:
- Proximity to populations susceptible to recruitment
- Local economic conditions making evil employment attractive
- Cultural factors normalizing service to villainous causes
- Discretion of local population regarding unusual employers

3. Training Facilities:
- Space for combat training, weapons qualification, indoctrination
- Obstacle courses and simulation environments
- Testing grounds for experimental equipment
- Isolation for specialized programs

4. Medical and Welfare:
- Healthcare capacity for workplace injuries: laser burns, hero encounters
- Mental health support for existential crises
- Family accommodation for long-term retention
- Retirement planning options

5. Loyalty Maintenance:
- Isolation preventing outside influence
- Surveillance capability for identifying traitors
- Limited communication with outside world
- Environmental factors creating dependency on the organization

Output: A vector of 5 probabilities [Exceptional, Strong, Adequate, Limited, Insufficient] summing to 1.
